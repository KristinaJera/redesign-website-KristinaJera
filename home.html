Grouping of catering wholesalers

Offering quality products at the best possible prices. That is the mission of Resto-Frit and Resto-Culinair. Fifteen wholesalers are grouped together under this flag, who together supply many hundreds of catering establishments. The members of Resto-Frit and Resto-Culinair all guarantee punctual and personal service. The members are all catering wholesalers with a family background and approach. No-nonsense companies that know perfectly what the real needs in the catering industry are. And that is why we want and can deliver customized solutions. In terms of service and contact, they always do better than the impersonal large chains. It is precisely because of the collaboration within Resto-Frit and Resto-Culinair that they can offer top products at very competitive prices. Resto-Frit has been around for 29 years now. During that time, the group grew into a top player in the sector, with an excellent reputation. Over the years, numerous profitable concepts and marketing campaigns have been developed. The best known is without a doubt the Smulllbox. In close collaboration with Studio 100, a handy box with deep-fried snacks was created that has been achieving staggering sales figures for years. Resto-Culinair, the culinary younger brother of Resto-Frit, focuses on a partnership with the more culinary catering business. Resto-Culinair serves both the friendly brasserie and the top restaurant.Please feel free to contact one of our wholesalers for additional explanation. Or visit our annual fair in Flanders Expo.
